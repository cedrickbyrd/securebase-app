# ✅ Pre-LinkedIn Post Checklist

## Before You Post

- [ ] Test demo URL works: https://demo.securebase.tximhotep.com
- [ ] Test signup URL works: https://securebase.tximhotep.com/signup
- [ ] Copy LinkedIn post from LINKEDIN_POST_WITH_DEMO.txt
- [ ] Set calendar link in DM templates
- [ ] Clear 30 minutes on your calendar after posting
- [ ] Have DM templates ready to copy/paste

## Optimal Posting Time

**Tuesday, 9:00 AM CST** (Central Time - Dallas)

Why?
- Monday = people catching up from weekend
- Tuesday = highest engagement day
- 9am = coffee time = LinkedIn scrolling
- End of week = engagement drops

## Immediately After Posting (First 30 Minutes = Critical)

- [ ] Like your own post (signals engagement to algorithm)
- [ ] Respond to EVERY comment within 2 minutes
- [ ] Send DM to EVERY commenter within 5 minutes
- [ ] Engage with 5-10 other posts (boost your visibility)
- [ ] DO NOT leave LinkedIn for 30 minutes

## First 24 Hours

- [ ] Check Netlify analytics (how many visited demo?)
- [ ] Follow up with anyone who visited demo but didn't comment
- [ ] Track all engagement in spreadsheet
- [ ] Respond to all DMs same day

## First Week

- [ ] Post again (different angle) on Thursday
- [ ] Email warm contacts with demo link
- [ ] Book 3 demo calls minimum
- [ ] Close 1 pilot customer

## Success = 

✅ 50+ reactions on post
✅ 10+ demo visits
✅ 5+ DMs sent
✅ 2+ demo calls booked
✅ 1 customer in trial

If you hit these numbers, you've validated product-market fit.
Scale from there.
