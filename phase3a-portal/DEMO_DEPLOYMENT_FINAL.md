## 🚀 Deploy Now (3 Steps)

### Step 1: Open Netlify
Go to: https://app.netlify.com

### Step 2: Deploy
1. Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
2. **Drag the `dist/` folder** from your computer to the Netlify dashboard
3. Wait ~30 seconds for deployment

### Step 3: Configure Domain
1. After deployment, go to "Site settings"
2. Click "Domain management"
3. Click "Add domain alias"
4. Enter: `demo.securebase.tximhotep.com`
5. Click "Verify" and "Add domain"
