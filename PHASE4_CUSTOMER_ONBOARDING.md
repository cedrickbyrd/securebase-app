## Phase 1: Account Access (15 minutes)

### Step 1: Check Your Email

Look for email with subject: **"Welcome to SecureBase - Your Account is Ready!"**

```
From: SecureBase Team <welcome@securebase.com>
Subject: Welcome to SecureBase - Your Account is Ready!

Hi [Your Name],

Your SecureBase account is now live!

Account Details:
  AWS Account ID: 123456789012
  Organization: [Your Company]
  Tier: [Healthcare/Fintech/Government/Standard]
  Portal: https://portal.securebase.com

Login Credentials:
  Email: your@email.com
  Temporary Password: [See below]
  
Next Steps:
  1. Click login link
  2. Change your password
  3. Enable MFA (recommended)
  4. Complete onboarding checklist
```

### Step 2: First Login

1. Click the **"Login to Portal"** button in email
2. Enter your email and temporary password
3. You'll be prompted to create a new password

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 12 characters
- At least 1 uppercase letter
- At least 1 lowercase letter
- At least 1 number
- At least 1 special character (!@#$%^&*)

### Step 3: Enable MFA (Recommended)

**Two-Factor Authentication adds extra security:**

1. After login, you'll see: "Set up MFA for enhanced security"
2. Click **"Enable MFA"**
3. Choose method:
   - **Authenticator App** (recommended): Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password
   - **SMS**: Receive codes via text message

**Using Authenticator App:**
```
1. Open your authenticator app
2. Scan the QR code shown
3. Enter the 6-digit code
4. Save backup codes (in case you lose your phone)
5. Click "Enable MFA"
```

**Done!** You're now securely logged in. âœ…

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Can't log in
**Solution:**
1. Check email/password (case-sensitive)
2. Try "Forgot Password"
3. Check spam folder for reset email
4. Contact support@securebase.com

### Issue: MFA code not working
**Solution:**
1. Ensure phone/app time is synced
2. Try regenerating code
3. Use backup codes (saved during MFA setup)
4. Contact support to reset MFA

### Issue: Logo looks blurry
**Solution:**
1. Upload higher resolution (400x120px)
2. Use PNG with transparency
3. Or use SVG for perfect scaling

### Issue: Report not generating
**Solution:**
1. Check date range (data may not exist yet)
2. Verify account has data
3. Try different chart type
4. Contact support with report ID
