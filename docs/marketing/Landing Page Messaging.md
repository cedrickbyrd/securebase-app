SecureBase is an **executive-risk platform** for fintech, healthcare, and other regulated industries. Instead of relying on screenshots, spreadsheets, and manual follow-up, teams use SecureBase to generate trusted, tamper-evident evidence that stands up to auditors, customers, insurers, and boards.

### Reduce Audit and Procurement Delays

### Strengthen Executive Oversight

## Feature Framing
### Automated Collection
Capture evidence from AWS IAM, Kubernetes, and other critical systems without manual effort.

### Framework Validation
Map collected evidence to standards such as SOC 2, HIPAA, and FFIEC.

### Certification Workflow
Generate PDF reports, sign them with KMS, and anchor their hash to create a timestamped provenance chain.

### GRC Distribution
Push authenticated evidence into tools like Drata and Vanta to support continuous governance.
