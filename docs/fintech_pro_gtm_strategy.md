## Customer Segments

### Segment 1 (Primary): Licensed Texas Money Transmitters
- **Size:** ~850 active MTL holders in Texas (NMLS data)
- **Profile:** Fintech, neo-banks, crypto on/off ramps, remittance companies
- **Pain:** $40K–$60K per DOB exam, 200-hour evidence compilation
- **Budget:** $150K–$500K/year compliance spend
- **Target tier:** Fintech Pro ($7,500/mo), Fintech Elite ($12,000/mo)
- **Decision maker:** Chief Compliance Officer, VP Compliance
- **Buying cycle:** 30–60 days (budget is pre-approved for compliance tools)

### Segment 2 (Secondary): Texas Digital Asset Service Providers (DASPs)
- **Size:** ~200 active or pending TX DASP license holders
- **Profile:** Crypto exchanges, custody providers, DeFi protocol operators
- **Pain:** HB 1666 is new (2023) and no existing tools automate DASP compliance
- **Budget:** Similar to Segment 1
- **Target tier:** Fintech Elite ($12,000/mo with DASP controls)

### Segment 3 (Tertiary): Pre-Licensure Applicants
- **Size:** ~300 applicants in pipeline at any time (NMLS data)
- **Profile:** Fintechs applying for TX MTL, building compliance infrastructure
- **Pain:** "We need to demonstrate we have compliance controls in place"
- **Target tier:** Fintech Pro (start before first exam)
- **Value prop:** "Show DOB you're ready before day one"

## Sales Process

```
Outreach → Discovery Call (30 min) → Demo (45 min) → POC (2 weeks) → Close
```

**Qualification (MEDDIC):**
- **M**etrics: Current exam prep cost >$50K? Time >100 hours?
- **E**conomic buyer: CCO with budget authority?
- **D**ecision criteria: Must automate transaction evidence?
- **D**ecision process: Who else must approve?
- **I**dentify pain: Have they had a DOB MRA?
- **C**hampion: Is the CCO enthusiastic?

**Demo flow (45 minutes):**
1. Screen share: Show compliance status dashboard
2. Walk through TX-MT-R1 evidence (5 years of transaction records, auto-organized)
3. Show CTR/SAR viewer with FinCEN tracking IDs
4. Click "Generate Examiner Package" → show signed PDF/CSV in 30 seconds
5. Contrast with their current process: "How long does this take you today?"
6. ROI calculator: plug in their hours and exam count

**POC (2-week proof of concept):**
- Connect to customer's staging/test database (read-only)
- Run evidence collection for 1 month of test data
- Customer validates accuracy vs. their own records
- If accuracy ≥90% → close deal

## Competitive Response Playbook

### If Vanta announces Texas MT compliance
- Accelerate Fintech Elite multi-state features
- Emphasize "purpose-built" vs. "bolted on" — we have real customer examiners validating evidence
- Lock in annual contracts with existing customers

### If a law firm builds a competing product
- Emphasize automation vs. manual (their lawyers = our Lambda functions)
- Highlight cost: law firm = $500/hour, SecureBase = $7,500/month flat
- Customer reference: "Our CCO says…"

### If pricing is challenged
- Lead with ROI: "If we save you 180 hours × $250/hour = $45,000 per exam…"
- Offer annual prepay at 20% discount
- Never discount below $5,000/month (below that, margin suffers and it signals low value)
