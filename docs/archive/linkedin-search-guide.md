## Step-by-Step LinkedIn Process

1. **Go to LinkedIn Sales Navigator** (or regular search)
2. **Use filters:**
   - Location: Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex
   - Title: CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Infrastructure
   - Company headcount: 11-50, 51-200
   - Industry: Computer Software, Hospital & Healthcare, Financial Services

3. **Look for signals:**
   - Recent job changes (new role = buying mode)
   - Posts about SOC 2, HIPAA, compliance
   - Company recently raised funding
   - Hiring infrastructure engineers

4. **Save promising prospects to list**

5. **Connect with personalized note:**
```
   Hi [Name], noticed you're building [product] at [Company].
   We help Dallas healthcare/fintech companies deploy compliant 
   AWS in minutes instead of weeks. Worth a quick chat?
```
