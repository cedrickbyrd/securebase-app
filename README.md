## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does deployment actually take?**  
A: 7-10 minutes on average. Longest deployment in testing: 12 minutes. You'll receive email confirmation when complete.

**Q: Do I need AWS expertise to use SecureBase?**  
A: No. Our platform handles all AWS configuration automatically. You interact through our customer portal, not AWS Console.

**Q: Can I use my existing AWS account?**  
A: Yes. SecureBase integrates with your existing AWS Organization or creates a new one. Your data stays in your AWS account.

**Q: What if I outgrow SecureBase?**  
A: You can export your complete Terraform configuration anytime. No lock-in, no migration costs.

**Q: Is there really a free trial?**  
A: Yes! 30 days, full features, no credit card required. Deploy production workloads during trial.

**Q: How is this different from Terraform Cloud?**  
A: Terraform Cloud is infrastructure-as-code orchestration. SecureBase is compliance-first, multi-tenant platform with customer billing built-in. We use Terraform under the hood.

**Q: What compliance frameworks do you support?**  
A: CIS Foundations (Standard), SOC 2 Type II (Fintech), HIPAA (Healthcare), FedRAMP Baseline (Government). All controls automated.

**Q: Can I see customer references?**  
A: We're in pilot phase with 5 companies (healthcare, fintech). References available upon request after pilot completion (March 2026).

**Q: What happens to my data if SecureBase shuts down?**  
A: Your infrastructure is deployed to YOUR AWS account. You own it. Export Terraform config anytime. Zero data loss risk.

**Q: Do you support [specific compliance framework]?**  
A: We currently support SOC 2, HIPAA, FedRAMP, CIS. Custom frameworks available for enterprise customers. Contact sales@securebase.io.
