## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue:** Negative customer feedback on complexity
**Response:**
- Acknowledge feedback
- Point to simplified quick-start guide
- Offer personalized onboarding call
- Consider UI simplification in next sprint

**Issue:** Low adoption (<20% Week 1)
**Response:**
- Increase communication frequency
- Offer incentives (e.g., free training session)
- Conduct user research (why not adopting?)
- Simplify onboarding flow

**Issue:** Technical problems at launch
**Response:**
- Immediate communication (in-app + email)
- Transparent status updates every 2 hours
- Compensation if downtime > 4 hours
- Post-mortem analysis and prevention plan
